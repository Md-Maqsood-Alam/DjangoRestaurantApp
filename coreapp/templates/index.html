About Us


At The Chinese Point, we don't just make food. We make people happy. The Chinese Point started with the belief that every meal should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do.

Since our modest beginnings in 2019 with a little space in Delhi, The Chinese Point's development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Indo-Chinese takeout food. The Chinese Point has developed to incorporate four superb takeout areas in Delhi with additional to come sooner rather than later. Our group takes pride in the way that we can furnish our new and faithful clients with extraordinary tasting nourishment.

With more than 10 years of experience under our belt, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and tested service principles: We prepare food we are proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
